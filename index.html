About Us

Our Story: From Community Roots to Everyday Essentials

Jakemartin Domingo Retail Hub was established with a simple mission: to serve our local community with affordable and accessible everyday goods. Starting as a modest sari-sari style setup, we have grown into a full-service convenience store trusted by residents of Barangay San Miguel and beyond.

  • Community First: We prioritize the needs of our kapitbahay.
  • Quality Products: From household items to quick snacks, we offer only trusted brands.
  • Friendly Service: Our staff are always ready to assist with a smile.

At Jakemartin, we believe in creating a welcoming space where everyone feels at home—para kang nasa tindahan ni kuya!
